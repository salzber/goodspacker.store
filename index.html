Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to help you plan your relocation better.

Are there any hidden charges on moving day?
Absolutely not. The estimate provided to you after the virtual or physical survey is final. Charges only alter if you add unnotified items or extra drop-off locations on the day of the move.
Do you provide insurance for my belongings?
Yes! We offer a comprehensive transit insurance policy at a minimal premium (typically 1.5% to 3% of declared value). If an unforeseen accident occurs, you are fully covered for damages.
How far in advance should I book my move?
For smooth scheduling, we recommend booking at least 7-10 days in advance. However, for month-end dates which are incredibly busy, a 15-day prior notice is highly recommended.
