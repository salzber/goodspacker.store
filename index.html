Why Trust Relocify With Your Move?

We treat your belongings as if they were our own. Our rigorous safety protocols and premium packing techniques ensure a flawless transition from your old doorstep to the new.

1. Heavy-Duty 5-Layer Protection

Forget cheap, flimsy cardboard. Our professional packing crew uses industry-grade 5-layer corrugated sheets, shock-absorbent bubble wraps, and moisture-resistant stretch films to safeguard your items.

For highly fragile items like glassware, artwork, and expensive electronics, we build custom wooden crates. Furthermore, our loaders are trained in spatial optimization to prevent any friction inside the cargo holds during transit.

2. 24/7 Live Tracking & Verified Crew

Stay informed every step of the way. All Relocify trucks are fitted with state-of-the-art GPS systems. You will receive an instant tracking link via WhatsApp so you know exactly where your belongings are at any given moment.

Safety starts with the right people. Our drivers and handlers undergo stringent background verifications and extensive driving capability tests to ensure smooth sailing across state lines.

Got Questions? We've Got Answers.

Find out more about how we simplify the relocation process for you.

Will I be asked to pay unexpected fees on the day of the move?

Never. The quotation we provide after finalizing your inventory is completely binding. Additional charges will only apply if you significantly increase the number of items or add unnotified drop-off locations mid-transit.

Is there financial protection if something breaks?

Yes, we offer comprehensive transit insurance at a very nominal premium. In the rare event of unforeseen accidents on the highway, your goods are completely covered, and our dedicated support team will help you settle claims swiftly.

How early should I book my relocation slot?

To ensure availability, especially during month-ends or weekends, we strongly advise booking your move at least 4 to 7 days in advance. This gives us ample time to arrange the perfect vehicle and packing crew for your requirements.
