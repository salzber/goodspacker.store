Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

At GoodsPacker Logistics, safeguarding your personal privacy is one of our highest priorities. We are fully committed to protecting the sensitive information you entrust to us during your relocation journey.

1. Data We Collect

When you request a moving estimate through our digital portal, we gather essential details required to process your request. This includes your Full Name, Mobile Number, and specific transit requirements (Current Location and Final Destination). This allows us to generate a highly accurate and customized quotation.

2. Purpose of Data Usage

The information we collect is utilized strictly to facilitate your moving experience. We use your contact details to share pricing estimates, coordinate logistical schedules, and enhance our overall service delivery. GoodsPacker Logistics maintains a strict zero-tolerance policy regarding data brokering—we never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third-party marketers.

3. Information Security Protocols

We deploy robust, industry-standard digital and physical security measures to ensure your personal data remains entirely safe from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or accidental destruction.

4. Use of Cookies

To continuously improve our platform, our website may employ cookies. These small data files help us analyze visitor traffic patterns, understand user preferences, and ultimately deliver a smoother, more optimized browsing experience tailored to your needs.
