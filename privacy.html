Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 13, 2026

At GoodsPacker, securing your privacy is paramount. We understand that moving involves sharing sensitive personal and logistical details. This document outlines our steadfast commitment to protecting the information you entrust to us throughout your relocation journey.

1. Information We Collect

When you engage with our digital platform to request a moving estimate, we gather specific details necessary to execute your request accurately. This typically includes your Full Name, Mobile Number, Email Address, and spatial requirements (Origin and Destination addresses). This data enables our dispatch team to generate highly precise, custom logistics quotations.

2. How We Utilize Your Data

Your information serves a singular purpose: facilitating a flawless moving experience. We utilize your contact details exclusively to communicate pricing, coordinate transit schedules, and provide real-time GPS tracking updates. GoodsPacker maintains a strict zero-tolerance stance on data brokering—we will never sell, rent, lease, or trade your personal information to third-party marketing entities.

3. Security & Protection Protocols

We do not compromise on digital safety. GoodsPacker deploys industry-leading encryption and robust physical security measures to ensure your data remains impenetrable. Access to your logistics information is strictly limited to verified operational personnel who require it to fulfill your moving services.

4. Cookie Policy & Analytics

To continually elevate our user experience, the GoodsPacker website utilizes small data files known as cookies. These files assist us in analyzing visitor traffic patterns, understanding user preferences, and optimizing our platform's performance to deliver a faster, more tailored browsing environment.
